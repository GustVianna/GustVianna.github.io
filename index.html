Personal Projects

Jello Quest

A mysterious infection is transforming the jellies into hideous creatures.
Adventure through the universe of Jello Quest and save your gelatinous companions as you try to discover what is happening with the sweet kingdom.

Magic Realm

Magic Realm is a 3D RTS, with low poly graphics, where you control all strategies in your battle against magic.
This game was produced as a project to conclude the Digital Games course for FATEC Americana.

Kitty Cubes

Help the cat to push his box into the right place and go to the next level.
Take on these challenges in a simple and relaxing way. You can change the music and also choose the skin you prefer for your cat.

VOCÊ.

A simulation of a person's daily life and their recurring activities, where each choice affects levels of quality of your life.
This game is developed for BGS Jam 2020.

Tamagotchi Hell

Remember the time when you couldn't stop thinking about the good old bullet hells you played on a tamagotchi?... Well, neither i but what about being locked inside one while you try to keep yourself alive? This game is developed for Bullet Hell Jam 2022.

Keep on Rolling

Help our dung beetles carry their dung heaps to the dung temple! Each level you complete brings a new challenge, new beetles and a greater amount of dung that will make the trajectory of our husky heroes. This game is develop for Ludijam.
